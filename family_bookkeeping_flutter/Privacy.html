Privacy Policy

Family Bookkeeping App
Effective Date: October 17, 2025

Quick Summary: We respect your privacy and only collect data necessary to provide our family bookkeeping services. We never sell your personal information and use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and family member details
  • Financial Data: Expenses, income, mileage, and hours tracked
  • Family Data: Family member names, relationships, and contact information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and session duration
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, and crash reports
  • Location Data: Only when you enable location services for expense tracking

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Service Delivery: Provide bookkeeping and expense tracking features
  • Family Management: Enable family member collaboration and data sharing
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your data across devices securely
  • Reports & Analytics: Generate financial reports and insights

2.2 Secondary Purposes

  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.2 Limited Sharing

  • Family Members: Share data only with authorized family members you've added
  • Service Providers: Share with trusted partners who help us operate the app (cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: Disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with notice)

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure, monitored servers with regular security updates
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication for all data access
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing

4.2 Your Responsibility

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Keep your device secure with screen locks and regular updates
  • Log out from shared or public devices
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately

5. Data Retention

5.1 Retention Periods

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance
  • Backup Data: Secure backups retained for up to 1 year for disaster recovery

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Access all your personal information through the app
  • Update Information: Modify your profile and family member details
  • Export Data: Download your data in CSV/PDF formats
  • Delete Data: Remove specific entries or entire account

6.2 Privacy Settings

  • Location Services: Enable/disable location tracking for expenses
  • Camera Access: Control camera permissions for receipt photos
  • Notifications: Manage push notification preferences
  • Data Sharing: Control which family members can see your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for family use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Other appropriate transfer mechanisms

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email notification
  • Displaying a notice in the app

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
